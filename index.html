About Me

I am a Lab Manager and Clinical Research Assistant in Dr. Anthony Kingâ€™s Mindfulness Lab at The Ohio State University Department of Psychiatry and Behavioral Health. My work bridges clinical practice and neuroscience, with a focus on how mindfulness-based interventions influence functional network connectivity and clinical outcomes in people with PTSD and Treatment-Resistant Depression.
